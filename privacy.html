Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Steadfast Solutions operates the InstaInvoice mobile application. This page informs you of the policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use this service.

Data Collection and Use

InstaInvoice is designed to function as an offline tool. No invoice data, client lists, or financial records are collected or stored on external servers. All data entered into the app remains stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and updates.
  • AdMob: Used to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve relevant ads.

Security

Since data is stored locally, its security depends on your device's security settings. It is recommended to use device encryption and screen locks to protect your information.

Contact

For questions regarding this policy, please use the contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.
