Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Steadfast Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") operates the InstaInvoice mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

Data Collection and Use

InstaInvoice is designed to function primarily as an offline tool. We do not collect or store your invoice data, client lists, or financial records on our servers. All data entered into the app remains stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and updates.
  • AdMob: Used to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and location data for personalized advertising.

Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since your data is stored locally, its security depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using device encryption and screen locks to protect your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support link on the Google Play Store.
